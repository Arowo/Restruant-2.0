About Us

The Spot Restaurant offers a fine dining experience in a stylish world class setting, and serves authentic Chinese meals with a touch of American influence. Ibachi’s teppanyaki / hibachi cirque is a melting pot of disparate rhythms, interests and cultures, and our restaurant also offers a contemporary 100 seater events-center on the second floor, to cater for birthday celebrations, corporate events, private business lunches and dinners, product launches, conferencing and holiday parties. We invite you to venture into our restaurant and join us for whatever takes your fancy – innovative flavours and fresh, vibrant, sumptuous dishes, or delightful company.

OUR STORY.

The Spot reflects the true heart of our restaurant through an elevated all day dining experience. Our name is a subtle nod to our central Leda sculpture. Dabbling is the graceful method in which swans submerge their heads under water to eat vegetation. Start your day with a hearty breakfast buffet, order from the a la cart menu, or grab a coffee and pastries on the go. Lunch features a thoughtful selection of small plates and creative light entrees. Let our barista prepare your favorite beverage as an afternoon treat. Our centrally located bar is the heartbeat of our space where guests and locals gather throughout the day. At night, the lights dim and Dabble transforms into an evening hot spot with comfort food and tasty cocktails.
